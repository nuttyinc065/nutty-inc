Why NuttyOS

All the Windows comforts with a Nutty twist.

Built to feel familiar from the moment you click Start: streamlined windows, smart snapping, and a crisp taskbar you already know how to use.

Windowing that makes sense

Drag, snap, and stack your workspaces with pixel-perfect alignment. NuttyOS keeps your windows organized with subtle depth and shadow cues.

Start menu that gets you

Pin what matters, filter by activity, and jump into curated work modes without digging through folders.

Taskbar ready for focus

A centered tray, soft glyphs, and live status updates keep you informed without stealing attention from your work.

Beautiful and accessible

High-contrast palettes, keyboard-friendly navigation, and crisp typography make NuttyOS comfortable day or night.
